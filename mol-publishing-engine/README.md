# Repository Structure

assets/
Brand assets, logos, icons, UI kits, templates, graphics, and reusable resources.

content/
Videos, tutorials, blog posts, newsletters, product announcements, and educational content.

products/
Digital products including templates, prompt packs, playbooks, and downloadable resources.

prompts/
Reusable AI prompts for development, marketing, analytics, and customer support.

scripts/
Automation scripts using Python, VBA, PowerShell, and other tooling.

automation/
Publishing workflows, deployment helpers, exports, and automation pipelines.

projects/
Current initiatives, completed work, experiments, and archived projects.

exports/
Final deliverables ready for customers or publication.

docs/
Documentation, SOPs, product planning, and technical references.

branding/
Brand identity, design standards, and messaging.

checklists/
Quality assurance, launch, and publishing checklists.
