# Repository Structure

assets/
Brand assets, fonts, icons, templates, music, stock resources, and reusable graphics.

content/
Scripts, captions, newsletters, social media, podcasts, and educational content.

products/
Digital planners, journals, challenges, affirmation packs, and printable resources.

prompts/
Reusable AI prompts for writing, wellness, marketing, and design.

scripts/
Automation scripts using VBA, Python, and PowerShell.

automation/
Publishing workflows, exports, and repetitive automation.

projects/
Current launches, campaigns, collaborations, and archived work.

exports/
Finished files ready for publishing or selling.

docs/
Documentation, SOPs, planning, and strategy.

branding/
Brand identity, tone, messaging, and visual standards.

checklists/
Quality assurance and publishing checklists.
