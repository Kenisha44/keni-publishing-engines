# Repository Structure

assets/
Brand assets, templates, icons, fonts, branding resources.

content/
Scripts, articles, social content, and educational material.

products/
PowerPoint templates, digital products, downloadable resources.

prompts/
Reusable AI prompts.

scripts/
Automation using VBA, Python, and PowerShell.

automation/
Content exports, publishing, and repetitive workflows.

projects/
Active client work and internal initiatives.

exports/
Final presentation files and product deliverables.

docs/
Business documentation and SOPs.

branding/
Design standards and brand assets.

checklists/
Quality assurance and publishing checklists.
