# 📚 Keni Can Math (KCM) Publishing Engine

# Mission

Keni Can Math exists to help students become confident in mathematics through engaging, beginner-friendly, and visually clear educational content.

Every lesson, worksheet, video, app feature, and digital product should make math feel approachable, practical, and enjoyable.

---

To become one of the world's most trusted independent mathematics education brands by combining:

- High-quality instruction
- Beautiful educational design
- Interactive technology
- Artificial Intelligence
- Affordable learning resources

# Repository Structure

```

assets/
Brand assets, logos, fonts, music, templates, and reusable design resources.

content/
Scripts and production files for educational content.

products/
Digital products created for students, teachers, and parents.

prompts/
Reusable AI prompts.

scripts/
Automation including VBA, Python, and PowerShell.

automation/
Publishing and export automation.

projects/
Current and archived production projects.

exports/
Final deliverables ready for upload or sale.

docs/
Documentation and planning.

branding/
Brand guidelines.

checklists/
Quality assurance checklists.

```
